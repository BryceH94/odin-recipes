Description

This is my go-to work-from-home lunch meal when I don't have any leftovers or time. All it takes is a can of chickpeas and a little time to get a protein boost before I get back to a busy day.

Ingredients

  • 2 15.5 oz. cans chickpeas, rinsed, patted dry
  • 4 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2.5 cups chopped mixed tender herbs

Steps

  1. Place chickpeas in a large skillet or Dutch oven and add garlic and oil; season with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until chickpeas are crisped and some have split open (these will be the most delicious ones), 10â€“15 minutes.
  3. Remove from heat; stir in herbs.
